The service provided by the referring web page is a current representation of area weather, whose main purpose is to keep the general public informed via the internet during severe weather events. In addition, this server accesses in realtime a selection of current official weather observations, forecasts, and warnings from U.S. government sources for use by the community. This site is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Timely delivery of data and products from this site through the Internet is not guaranteed. Official National Weather Service dissemination systems which can provide timely delivery of data and products are listed below.

  • NOAA Weather Radio,
  • NOAA Weather Wire,
  • EMWIN®,
  • Family of Services (FOS),
  • NOAAPORT,
  • and your local National Weather Service.
